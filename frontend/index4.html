Women's Safety Tips & Modules

Tip 1: Share Your Location

Always share your live location with trusted contacts when traveling alone, especially at night.

Tip 2: Keep Emergency Contacts Ready

Have a list of emergency contacts saved for quick access. Many smartphones allow setting them on the lock screen.

Module: Self-defense Techniques

Learn basic self-defense techniques to protect yourself. Many online resources and courses are available for training.

Tip 3: Be Aware of Your Surroundings

Stay alert, especially in unfamiliar areas. Avoid distractions like using your phone while walking in public.
